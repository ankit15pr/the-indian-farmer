AGRICULTURE AND FARMERS WELFARE, HARYANA

Vegetables

Carrot

Timing: Spring to early summer

Varieties: Nantes, Danvers, Chantenay

Climate Required: Cool weather, 60-70°F (15-21°C)

Fertilizers Required: Balanced NPK, compost

Irrigation: Regular watering, keep soil consistently moist

Growth Period: 70-80 days

Yield Potential: 2-3 lbs per square foot

Potato

Timing: Early spring to mid-summer

Varieties: Russet, Yukon Gold, Red Pontiac

Climate Required: Cool temperatures, 45-75°F (7-24°C)

Fertilizers Required: Nitrogen-rich fertilizers, compost

Irrigation: Consistent moisture, avoid waterlogging

Growth Period: 70-120 days

Yield Potential: 10-20 lbs per plant

Tomato

Timing: Late spring to summer

Varieties: Roma, Cherry, Beefsteak

Climate Required: Warm weather, 70-85°F (21-29°C)

Fertilizers Required: Balanced NPK, compost

Irrigation: Regular watering, avoid overhead irrigation

Growth Period: 60-85 days

Yield Potential: 10-20 lbs per plant

Spinach

Timing: Spring and fall

Varieties: Bloomsdale, Tyee, New Zealand

Climate Required: Cool weather, 50-70°F (10-21°C)

Fertilizers Required: Nitrogen-rich fertilizers, compost

Irrigation: Regular watering, keep soil moist

Growth Period: 40-50 days

Yield Potential: 2-3 lbs per square foot

Broccoli

Timing: Spring or fall

Varieties: Calabrese, Romanesco, Broccoli Rabe

Climate Required: Cool weather, 60-70°F (15-21°C)

Fertilizers Required: Balanced NPK, compost

Irrigation: Regular watering, keep soil evenly moist

Growth Period: 70-100 days

Yield Potential: 2-3 lbs per plant

Fruits

Apple

Timing: Late summer to early fall

Varieties: Fuji, Gala, Honeycrisp

Climate Required: Temperate regions, 32-45°F (0-7°C) in winter

Fertilizers Required: Nitrogen-rich fertilizers, compost

Irrigation: Deep watering, avoid waterlogging

Growth Period: 6-8 months from flowering to harvest

Yield Potential: 300-600 lbs per tree

Banana

Timing: Year-round in tropical climates

Varieties: Cavendish, Plantain, Red Banana

Climate Required: Tropical, 75-95°F (24-35°C)

Fertilizers Required: High potassium fertilizers, compost

Irrigation: Regular watering, keep soil moist

Growth Period: 9-12 months from planting to harvest

Yield Potential: 30-40 lbs per plant

Orange

Timing: Winter to early spring

Varieties: Navel, Valencia, Blood Orange

Climate Required: Subtropical to tropical, 55-90°F (13-32°C)

Fertilizers Required: Citrus-specific fertilizers, compost

Irrigation: Deep watering, avoid waterlogging

Growth Period: 7-12 months from flowering to harvest

Yield Potential: 100-200 lbs per tree

Mango

Timing: Summer to early fall

Varieties: Alphonso, Haden, Tommy Atkins

Climate Required: Tropical, 77-95°F (25-35°C)

Fertilizers Required: High potassium and phosphorus fertilizers, compost

Irrigation: Regular watering, avoid waterlogging

Growth Period: 3-6 months from flowering to harvest

Yield Potential: 200-300 lbs per tree

Grapes

Timing: Late summer to early fall

Varieties: Concord, Thompson Seedless, Cabernet Sauvignon

Climate Required: Temperate to subtropical, 65-85°F (18-29°C)

Fertilizers Required: Balanced NPK fertilizers, compost

Irrigation: Regular watering, ensure good drainage

Growth Period: 4-6 months from flowering to harvest

Yield Potential: 10-20 lbs per vine

Floriculture

Rose

Timing: Late spring to fall

Varieties: Hybrid Tea, Floribunda, Climbing

Climate Required: Mild to warm climates, 65-75°F (18-24°C)

Fertilizers Required: Rose-specific fertilizers, compost

Irrigation: Regular watering, avoid wetting foliage

Growth Period: 2-3 months to bloom

Yield Potential: High, with continuous blooms

Sunflower

Timing: Late spring to early summer

Varieties: Giant, Dwarf, Teddy Bear

Climate Required: Warm weather, 70-85°F (21-29°C)

Fertilizers Required: Balanced NPK, compost

Irrigation: Regular watering, especially during dry spells

Growth Period: 70-90 days

Yield Potential: High, with multiple blooms per plant

Marigold

Timing: Spring to fall

Varieties: French, African, Signet

Climate Required: Warm weather, 65-75°F (18-24°C)

Fertilizers Required: Balanced fertilizers, compost

Irrigation: Regular watering, avoid waterlogging

Growth Period: 50-60 days

Yield Potential: High, with continuous blooms

Lily

Timing: Late spring to summer

Varieties: Asiatic, Oriental, Trumpet

Climate Required: Mild to cool weather, 60-70°F (15-21°C)

Fertilizers Required: Bulb fertilizers, compost

Irrigation: Regular watering, avoid waterlogging

Growth Period: 60-90 days

Yield Potential: High, with multiple flowers per bulb

Lotus

Timing: Summer

Varieties: Sacred Lotus, American Lotus

Climate Required: Warm climates, 70-90°F (21-32°C)

Fertilizers Required: Aquatic plant fertilizers, compost

Irrigation: Requires standing water, keep soil submerged

Growth Period: 70-120 days

Yield Potential: High, with multiple blooms per plant
