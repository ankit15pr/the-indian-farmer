Wheat

Timing: November to April

Soil Required: Loamy soil

Varieties: HD 2967, PBW 343

Climate: Cool and dry climate, 12-25°C

Fertilizers: Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Potassium

Diseases: Rust, Powdery mildew, Leaf blight

Irrigation : Moderate, avoid waterlogging

Growth Period: 120-150 days

Yield Potential: 2400-2800 kg

Rice

Timing: June to November

Soil Required: Clayey soil

Varieties: Basmati, Jasmine, Japonica

Climate: Hot and humid, 20-40°C

Fertilizers: Urea, Phosphates

Diseases: Bacterial blight, Blast, Brown spot

Irrigation : High, requires standing water

Growth Period: 100-180 days

Yield Potential: 2200-2400 kg

Mustard

Timing: October to March

Soil Required: Loamy to sandy loam soil

Varieties: Pusa Mustard, NRCHB-506

Climate: Cool and dry, 15-25°C

Fertilizers: Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Potassium

Diseases: White rust, Alternaria blight

Irrigation: Low to moderate

Growth Period: 90-120 days

Yield Potential: 760-850 kg

Cotton

Timing: April to September

Soil Required: Black soil or sandy loam

Varieties: BT Cotton, Hybrid Cotton

Climate: Warm, 25-35°C

Fertilizers: Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Potash

Diseases: Wilt, Leaf Curl Virus

Irrigation : Moderate, drought tolerant

Growth Period: 150-180 days

Yield Potential: 800-1000 kg

Millet(Bajra)

Timing: June to September

Soil Required: Sandy to loamy soil

Varieties: HHB 67, Pusa Composite

Climate: Hot, semi-arid, 25-35°C

Fertilizers: Nitrogen, Phosphorus

Diseases: Downy mildew, Ergot

Irrigation : Low, drought tolerant

Growth Period: 75-90 days

Yield Potential: 2200-2400 kg

Sugarcane

Timing: November to April

Soil Required: Well-drained loamy soil

Varieties: Co-0238, Co-86032

Climate: Warm, 25-30°C

Fertilizers: Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Potassium

Diseases: Red Rot, Smut

Irrigation : High, requires regular irrigation

Growth Period: 10-18 months

Yield Potential: 80-120 tons per hectare
